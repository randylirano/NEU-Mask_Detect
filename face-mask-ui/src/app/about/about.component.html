Meet KATI

KATI is a publicly accessible web-based tool that makes it easier for anyone, including businesses, organizations, or public spaces, to determine whether a person is following mask-wearing protocols. This is all accomplished through the magic of AI, which reduces the need for human interaction during a global pandemic.
